A portfolio that generates passive income every month.

A portfolio that generates passive income every month.

Have you ever thought about having a source of passive income that would bring you money without your participation?

Someone imagines it in the form of a money tree, on which fruits are blooming in monetary form. You are sleeping, and your money tree is bearing fruit.

In the morning, you collect money from your tree and spend it. Or don't spend it.

Someone imagines it as a hen laying Golden eggs.

I think of it as right the completed portfolio.

In one of my articles, I already mentioned that this year I will create a "source" that would bring me passive income.

Initially, I included only shares that pay dividends in such a portfolio.

But the summer was over, and with it, too the dividend season. All the dividends have been received and you don't have to wait until next year for the balance to be replenished.

And so I want to have a passive income every month!

And there is a way out of this situation. The passive income portfolio should include not only shares, but also corporate bonds and OFZ (Federal loan bonds) in equal shares. Moreover, this portfolio will also be well balanced.

How do I create such a portfolio ? You must include bonds that pay coupons in different months. That's right the selected bonds in this portfolio will yield an income every month.

I will tell you about which bonds I have already purchased for my portfolio and which ones I still plan to buy, in one of my next articles.

You can find out what stocks are in my portfolio here.

That's all for today.
