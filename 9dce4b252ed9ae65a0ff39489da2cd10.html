Mutual funds or shares: what to choose for a novice investor.

Mutual funds or shares: what to choose for a novice investor.

Well, now you have a free amount of money. And of course you want to dispose of them properly, invest them so that they bring income. You have decided to enter the stock market market. But how to do this?

You may have already heard that there are mutual funds of stocks and there are shares, but what is the difference between them? And what is the best choice to choose? It all depends on you: how you plan to manage your portfolio - actively or passively. How much money are you going to start with? And also for how long you are going to invest tools.

Let's first understand what mutual funds and shares are.

Mutual funds-mutual funds are a form of collective investment, in which the owners own some share in the Fund. That is, people they transfer a certain amount of money under the management of the management company, and the management company, having collected a sufficient amount, invests in what is written out in their contract. You do not need to enter the stock market, the UK will do everything for you.

Shares are the purchase of a share of the company itself. In this case you are creating your own stock portfolio independently.

Mutual funds are convenient because the minimum entry threshold is 1000-5000 rubles. Your funds are managed by a management company, which charges a certain percentage of the amount. You can only observe how the management system works.

Mutual funds are different: mutual funds of stocks, bonds, mixed investments (i.e. both stocks and bonds), there are mutual funds of certain sectors (oil and gas, electricity, etc.), and foreign funds. If you do not know what to choose, then you can limit yourself to an index Fund (mutual Fund MICEX), which buys shares of leading companies Russian company.

The list of funds can be found on the website investfunds.ru. Here you can also view the rating of funds by return for a year or three years, and choose what you like best. But remember! Good returns in the past do not guarantee the same returns in the future. Therefore, do not be lazy, study how long ago the management company works in the market, what percentage it takes for management. After all, you give your money to other people.

One of the disadvantages of mutual funds, I believe, is the inability to receive dividends on shares,since all dividends are reinvested again in shares. But this, in turn, increases the cost pifs'.

If buying mutual funds is passive inventing, then buying shares is active inventing. In this case, the cost of your portfolio will depend on you.

If you decide to invest your money in shares yourself, then you will first need to carefully and carefully study the brokers. It is especially important to pay attention to the Commission fee. Each broker has its own commissions.

First of all, this is the percentage of transactions made on the market. Almost all brokers have this feature. Then there is the Deposit fee. some brokers take it only in the month when the transactions were made, others take it every month, regardless of the transaction. If you are not going to trade every month, then the monthly Deposit fee can "eat" all your money, especially if the initial amount was not large. And then there are the brokers, they charge a Commission if your account balance is less than the minimum amount set by the broker.

All these commissions can significantly reduce the cost of your portfolio on a monthly basis!

Once you have decided on a broker, you will still have to independently study the stock market and, of course,, to learn quik (program for working in the background market). Therefore, be prepared to allocate free time for this activity.

A significant advantage of investing in shares is the receipt of dividends. But not all companies pay dividends based on the stocks. And again, there are banks that charge a Commission for withdrawing these dividends, so sometimes it is better to reinvest them.

Therefore, wherever you decide to invest your money, take the time and study this area thoroughly. And take the time to manage your finances at least once a week.

Successful thank you for your investment!
